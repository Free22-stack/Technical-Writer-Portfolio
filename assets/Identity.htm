3 Squares Vermont Program Manual

Identity

Verify the identity of the person applying.

Verify the identity of an authorized representative applying on behalf of a household and the head of household.

Identity is the only mandatory verification when providing expedited service. See Expedited Service, Verification of Identity.

Verification

Verify identity using readily available documentary evidence, or if unavailable, through a collateral contact. Accept any documents which establish the applicant or authorized representative’s identity.

Note: Numident for persons with an SSN verifies identity.

Application

Deny the application if not verified by the due date.

Verify identity before providing expedited service.
