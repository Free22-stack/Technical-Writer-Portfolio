Case Management

Overview

Management Evaluation (ME) reviewers monitor programs access and operations. The reviewers:

  • Review case files for accuracy and completeness
  • Interview staff, community agencies, and recipients
  • Review application process and office procedures
  • Complete a final report identifiying best practices and reccomendations for improvement

 

Scope

Active 3SVT cases are randomly selected for quality control review. The review determines:

  • Eligibility and benefit accuracy.

  • Timeliness of the action for certification within the current Federal Fiscal Year.

  • Corrective action recommendations.
